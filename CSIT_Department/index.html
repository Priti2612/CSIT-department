About Information Technology

The Department of Information Technology was started in the year 2001 with the intake of 60 students. From year 2019-20 department has offering B. Tech. in Computer Science & Information Technology.

Our training And placement department works hard to make students liable to face interviews. Due to this efforts last three year’s placement is more than 90%. Highest package offered is 15 Lakh per annum. Department received Rs. 1 Lakh fund from Unnat Bharat Abhiyan for research project proposal “Villagers Grievance Redressal: E- Complaint Management System". Also received Rs. 3.10 Lakh from Rajiv Gandhi Science & Technology Commission for project “Company Connect”.

Department having MoU with PRISTINE Info Solution Pvt. Ltd., Pune to develop expertise in the area cyber security. MoU with Eduskills Foundation Technology, Bhubaneswar, MITU Skillologies, Pune and Emtec Technologies Pvt. Ltd., Pune to develop expertise in the area machine learning and support for internship. Department developed virtual lab for course Computer Network Laboratory. Department promotes research in Machine Learning, Internet of Things, Cyber Security.

Mission

  • To offer high quality education through state of art curriculum and innovative teaching & learning practices.
  • To establish state of art laboratories and center of excellence in the field of technology.
  • To adopt professional practice, standards and values.
  • To inculcate problem solving aptitude in graduates with lifelong learning skills to become valuable resource for IT industry and society.
  • To create, share, and apply knowledge in Computer Science and Information Technology, including in interdisciplinary areas that extend the scope of Computer Science and Information Technology to benefit society.
